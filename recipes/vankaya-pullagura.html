Vankaya Pullagura

Description

Vankaya pullagura is a South Indian vegetable dish with eggplant. It's great if you have big eggplants, rather than the small ones used in gutti vankaya.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and urad dal
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 green chili pepper

  • 1 diced eggplant
  • 2 medium-sized tomato
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan on low to medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and urad dal for tempering. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add diced onions and green chilies. Fry until translucent and golden.
  4. Add diced eggplant and salt. Cook until it becomes soft and mash some of the eggplant pieces.
  5. Add diced tomates to pan and cook until most of the liquid evaporates.
  6. Remove from heat and mix in lemon juice.
  7. Serve with rice or chapati.
