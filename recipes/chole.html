Chole

Description

Chole (cho-lay) is a chickpea tomato curry. It's a cheap and quick meal for college students. You can enjoy it with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 green chili pepper
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 2 medium-sized tomato
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan on low to medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle cumin seeds and bay leaf on the pan. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add diced onions and green chilies. Fry until translucent and golden.
  4. Mix in ginger-garlic paste for a few minutes.
  5. Add diced tomates to pan. When tomatoes are mushy, add garam masala, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  6. When most of the liquid is evaporated from pan, add can of chickpeas (including liquid) and cook until soft.
  7. Mash some of the chickpeas with a wooden spoon.
  8. Remove from heat and mix in lemon juice.
  9. Garnish with cilantro and serve warm.
