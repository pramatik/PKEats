Chicken Keema

Description

Chicken Keema is ground chicken cooked with Indian spices. It's a quick and simple meal that packs a lot of protein. Perfect to eat with naan or even as taco meat!

Feel free to swap the chicken out for turkey, lamb, or plant-based options.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 green chili pepper
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 medium-sized tomato
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan on low to medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle cumin seeds and bay leaf on the pan. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add diced onions and green chilies. Fry until translucent and golden.
  4. Mix in ginger-garlic paste for a few minutes.
  5. Add minced meat to onion mixture and cook until white. Sprinkle salt and turmeric and mix.
  6. Add diced tomates to pan. When tomatoes are mushy, add garam masala and red chili powder.
  7. When most of the liquid is evaporated from pan, add frozen peas and cook until soft.
  8. Serve warm with naan.
